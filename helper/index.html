  1. How to add new Quiz ?

    1. Click “Site Home” from left sidebar

    2. Click on Settings icon from the gear in the right side of the page

    3. Click on “Turn editing on”

    4. Click “add an activity or resource”

    5. Choose “quiz”

    6. Click “add”

    7. fill the fields

    8. Click “Save & display”

  2. How to edit Quiz ?

    1. Click on Settings icon from the gear in the right side of the page

    2. Then click on edit settings

    3. Edit what you need

    4. Then click on save

  3. How to add new Question ?

    1. After adding your quiz

    2. Click on “edit Quiz”

    3. Click “Add” from this link in the bottom then “a new question”

    4. Choose the type of questions

    5. click “add”

    6. Fill the fields

    7. Then click on save & return

  4. How to remove Quiz ?

    1. Click on the edit link beside the quiz name

    2. Click on delete

    3. Click on Yes in the confirmation message

  5. How to remove question ?

    1. Click on delete icon which beside the question name and text

    2. Click on Yes in the confirmation message

  1. How to attempt quiz ?

    1. Click on the quiz name which inside of your topic or course

    2. Click on attempt quiz

    3. Answers the questions

    4. Check if the quiz has pages of questions

    5. Click on finish after answering all questions

    6. Then click on Yes to show your grade
